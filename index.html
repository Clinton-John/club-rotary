About Us

The Kabarak University Rotary Club is a vibrant chapter dedicated to humanitarian service, integrity, and advancing world understanding. As part of Rotary International, we receive support from the United Nations to amplify our efforts in community development.

Our club focuses on education, health, and economic empowerment, with members from diverse backgrounds uniting for meaningful change.

  • Founded: 2015 at Kabarak University
  • Members: 50+ active volunteers
  • UN Partnership: Collaborating on sustainable development goals

Our Projects

We have completed various community projects, including visiting orphanages, educational workshops, health camps, and environmental initiatives.

Orphanage Visits

Regular visits to local orphanages providing supplies, mentorship, and joy to children in need.

Education Workshops

Free workshops for underprivileged youth, focusing on skills development and career guidance.

Health Camps

Organizing medical camps offering free check-ups and awareness on preventive healthcare.

Environmental Initiatives

Tree planting drives and clean-up campaigns to promote sustainability in our community.
